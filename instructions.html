How to Add Perplexity as a Search Engine

  1. Open Chrome Settings.
  2. Go to the "Search engine" section.
  3. Click "Manage search engines and site search".
  4. Click "Add" next to "Site search".
  5. Fill in the details:
    • Search engine: Perplexity
    • Keyword: `
    • URL with %s in place of query: https://www.perplexity.ai/search?q=%s
  6. Click "Add" to save.

Once added, you can use the shortcut "`" to quickly search using Perplexity from the address bar.
